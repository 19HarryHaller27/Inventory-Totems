# Inventory-Totems

This mod is designed to give you a multitude of mod tools you can use. A way to register item relationships between each other within your inventory and allow those items to do something. A way for items in inventory to give you stats. A way for items to interact with eachother in your inventory. And a way for you to randomly assign visuals on 'pick up' or 'grab' among designated item populations.
